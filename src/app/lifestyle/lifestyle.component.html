Natural Lifestyle

Lifestyle Teacher: Emma Berry

Several years ago Emma was diagnosed with an allergy to propylene glycol. Before her diagnosis this chemical was not even on our radar. When we began investigating, we found it was in everything from shampoo to chocolate muffins! Concern for her health led us on a natural living journey which has reaped so many benefits for our whole family. We love to share what we have learned about organic foods, cooking from scratch, homemade beauty products, and more.
