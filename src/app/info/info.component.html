About Us

Hi! We are Carol and Emma, mother and daughter, excited to share with you the joys we have found in homeschooling and natural living. We began our homeschool journey when Emma started first grade and one brother was in third grade and another brother was just a new baby. We partnered with many other families over the years to create adventures which inspire learning, strengthen families, and teach core values. Along the way we have talked with so many parents and students who are looking for educational options, but feel overwhelmed or discouraged or unprepared. We want to empower families, parents, and students to embark on great education adventures and help them find joy all along the way!
