Welcome to Joyful Journeys!

A Joyful Journey is a year long thematic curriculum. We choose a theme each year that we feel will be fun and exciting for our children and for us. Sometimes the idea comes from a favorite movie or book. Sometimes the idea comes from a subject we really want to study. Sometimes the idea comes from a Core value we really want to teach. Every year is different, exciting and tailored to the needs of our families at that specific time. Joyful Journeys are exciting adventures that we go on every year as we homeschool our children.
